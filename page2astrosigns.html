Elements

Elements in astrology are tied to specific personality traits and tendencies.

There are 4 elements: Fire, Earth, Air, Water

  • Fire signs are strong, creative, enthusiastic, self-assured, and fun.

  • Earth signs are grounded, pragmatic, perceptive, epicurean, and forthright.

  • Air signs are intellectual, communicative, sociable, witty, and fair.

  • Water signs are intuitive, receptive, empathetic, imaginative, and emotional.

Modalities

Modalities are what represent the unique ways each group of signs express their energy, reacts to circumstances and navigates through life.

There are 3 modalities: Cardinal, Fixed, and Mutable

  • Cardinal signs are the natural leaders of the zodiac. They are independent and have a lot of drive and ambition.
    Their biggest fear is not being in control.

  • Fixed signs value stability and are determined and resolute. They are very reliable and persistent.
    Their biggest fear is change.

  • Mutable signs are adaptable and flexible. They are very resourceful, sharp, and sympthetic.
    Their biggest fear is commitment.
